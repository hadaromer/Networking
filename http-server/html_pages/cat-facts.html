5 Interesting Facts About Cats

  • Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four toes on their back paws.
  • The average cat sleeps for about 12-16 hours a day.
  • Cats have a unique grooming pattern called "allogrooming," where they use their tongue to clean each other's fur.
  • The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave on the Mediterranean island of Cyprus.
  • Cats use their whiskers to measure openings and spaces. If a cat can fit its whiskers through a space, the rest of its body can usually fit through as well.
