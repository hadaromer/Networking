5 Interesting Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, about 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans.
  • The Basenji dog breed is known as the "barkless dog" because it doesn't bark like other dogs; instead, it makes a unique yodel-like sound.
  • Dogs have three eyelids. The third eyelid, known as the nictitating membrane or "haw," helps keep the eye moist and protected.
  • The world's oldest known breed is the Saluki, originally bred in Egypt around 329 B.C.
  • Dogs belong to the Canidae family, which also includes wolves, foxes, and other animals.
